Mechanics is a broader branch of Physics that includes kinematics(force and motion) and dynamics.

Dynamics is what causes motion to happen. In other words, it is the force that acts on something to produce motion. If I was to push a box, my force acting upon the box will by the dynamic that will eventually end up moving the box(motion). This is Dynamics in simple terms.
F=ma Force = mass * acceleration
